Sustainability Project Funding

What types of funding are available?

Funding Types

Grants

Grants provide funding for a project or initiative through a grant application process.

Programs

Programs listed provide funding with the expectation that the applicants participate in a specific program or initiative to receive the funding.

Awards

Awards listed would provide funding to those nominated for an award based on past work or accomplishments, with the thought that students can apply that award money to the projects they are seeking to fund.

Funding Categories

BIPOC

This funding category prioritizes applications for projects or initiatives led by individuals/teams who are Black, Indigenous, and/or people of colour.

Clean Energy

This funding opportunity prioritizes applications for projects or initiatives that involve renewable energy technology or carbon emission reduction.

Climate

This funding category prioritizes applications for projects or initiatives related to climate solutions.

Community

This funding category prioritizes applications for projects or initiatives that support community building or are by-and-for a particular community.

Emergency/Safety

This funding category prioritizes applications that focus on crisis response and community safety. These would be applicable for projects related to extreme weather events and other climate adaptation measures.

Youth

This funding category prioritizes applications from individuals under 30 years old and/or projects or initiatives that support individuals under 30 years old.
